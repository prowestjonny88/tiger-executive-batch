Safe Next Steps

Follow these recalibration steps to safely restore power delivery to your vehicle module.

warning

Safety Warning

Do not attempt to open the charging port cover or touch exposed pins if the grounding light is flashing amber. Ensure the surrounding area is dry before proceeding.

1

Engage Safety Lock

Depress the connector release button for 3 seconds until the blue LED turns solid.

2

Cycle Power Module

Toggle the physical switch on the pedestal base to the 'OFF' position, wait for 10 seconds, and return to 'ON'.

3

Rescan QR Code

Reconnect the cable and initiate the handshake sequence via the primary dashboard scanner.

info Reference: Standard ISO 15118 Communication Protocol
